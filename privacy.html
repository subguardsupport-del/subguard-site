Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

SubGuard ("we", "our", "the app") is designed from the ground up to be radically private. This policy explains, in plain English, exactly what happens to your data.

1. Data we collect

None that leaves your device. The app stores the following locally on your phone only:

  • The email address and password you enter to create your on-device account (the password is one-way hashed with a per-device salt; the plain password is never stored)
  • The subscriptions you add (name, cost, renewal date, category, notes)
  • Your preferences (currency, dark mode, PIN, notification settings)

None of the above is transmitted to SubGuard servers. We do not operate any backend that receives your subscription data.

2. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, or date of birth.
  • We do not request, access, or store any bank account or credit card information.
  • We do not embed any analytics SDK (no Firebase Analytics, no Amplitude, no Mixpanel).
  • We do not track your in-app behaviour, screen views, or usage patterns.

3. Third-party services

SubGuard integrates with the following third-party services, and only for the specific purposes listed:

  • Google AdMob — used to display a single banner ad on the Dashboard screen. AdMob may collect a mobile advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) to serve contextual ads. On iOS we request your permission via App Tracking Transparency before any identifier is used. You can disable personalised ads at any time via your device settings. See Google's ads policy.
  • Apple Push Notification Service / Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — used only to locally schedule renewal-reminder notifications. No content is sent through the APNs/FCM servers; scheduling happens entirely on-device.

4. Permissions SubGuard requests

  • Notifications — to remind you before your subscriptions renew. You may decline this and the app will still work; you just won't get reminders.
  • Biometrics (Face ID / Touch ID / Fingerprint) — optional. Used only to unlock the app locally.
  • Internet access — used only by AdMob to fetch the banner ad. No subscription data is ever transmitted.

5. Children's privacy

SubGuard is rated for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your rights

Because we store nothing on our servers, there is no data for us to export, correct, or delete on your behalf. To delete all of your data, open the app, go to Settings → Reset all data, or simply uninstall the app — everything is removed from your device.

7. Changes to this policy

If we change how SubGuard handles data, we will update this page and notify users in-app before the change takes effect. We will never retroactively reduce the privacy protections you signed up for.

8. Contact

Questions or concerns? Email subguard.support@gmail.com and we'll get back to you within 48 hours.
