Questions?

Everything else.

Is SubGuard really free?

Yes. The app is fully free, supported by a single non-intrusive banner ad on the dashboard. No signup, no paywall, no tricks.

Does SubGuard connect to my bank account?

No. SubGuard never asks for bank login or credit-card info. You enter subscription details manually (or with one tap from a preset). Everything stays on your phone.

What happens if I lose my phone?

Your subscription data is stored only on that device. You can re-enter them on a new phone. Cloud backup is coming in a future release as an optional opt-in.

Which currencies are supported?

USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD and INR out of the box. Change it anytime under Settings → Currency.

Why do I need to create a local account?

The email + password protects your data on-device even if someone else picks up your phone. It's never sent anywhere — the password is hashed with a per-device salt and never leaves the device.

Can I turn off the ad?

A one-time Premium upgrade that removes the banner is on the roadmap. Until then, the ad is a single small banner at the very bottom of the dashboard — no pop-ups, no video ads.
