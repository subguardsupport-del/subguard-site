SubGuard is built by a tiny team that personally reads every support email. Most questions are answered below — if yours isn't, just email us.

Quick help

I forgot my PIN.

Because SubGuard stores nothing on servers, we cannot reset a PIN for you. From the Lock screen, tap Sign out instead and log in again with your email + password. If you've also forgotten the password, the only option is Reset all data from the app (or uninstall + reinstall). Your subscription data is tied to your on-device account.

Reminders are not arriving on Android.

Android's battery-optimisation sometimes pauses background alarms. Go to: Android Settings → Apps → SubGuard → Battery → Unrestricted. Also make sure SubGuard has notification permission enabled at the system level.

Can I move my subscriptions to a new phone?

At launch SubGuard does not support device-to-device transfer — a deliberate trade-off for our offline-first privacy model. Encrypted backup/restore is planned for v1.2.

Why do I see an ad even though SubGuard is free?

A single non-intrusive banner ad at the bottom of the Dashboard pays the hosting + Play Store fee for the app. We don't show interstitials, pop-ups, or video ads. A one-time Premium upgrade to remove the banner is on the roadmap.

Does SubGuard work without internet?

Yes — everything except the banner ad works fully offline. Set up subscriptions, add reminders, view insights: no network needed.

Can I add more currencies?

Currently we support USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD and INR. If you need another, email us — we add them based on user demand.

I want to delete all my data.

Open the app, go to Settings → Reset all data and confirm. That wipes your account and every subscription from the device. Alternatively, just uninstall the app.
